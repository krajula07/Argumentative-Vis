Does Experience Really matters to get Placed?

Source: Kaggle Placements

What are placements?

Internships/full time opportunities which occur in college are generally called as placements.

Topic:

This visualization is basically explaining how work experience is important in placements,Where one chart says that work experience doesn't matter and the other says experience matters.

Left Chart:

Left chart takes number of people irrespective of all attributes and depicts experience doesn't matter

  • Rhetoric device : Anchoring
  • Rhetoric Method : Omission and Scaling

Right Chart:

Right chart takes ratio of people that is the percentage of people in all attributes and depicts experience does matter

  • Rhetoric device : Anchoring
  • Rhetoric Method : Omission and Scaling
